# Dutch Football Competition Rules

## Promotion and Relegation Rules

### Eredivisie (Tier 1)
- **Champion**: Qualifies for UEFA Champions League group stage
- **2nd place**: Qualifies for UEFA Champions League 3rd qualifying round
- **3rd place**: Qualifies for UEFA Europa League play-off round
- **4th-7th place**: Enter promotion/relegation playoffs for European qualification
- **16th place**: Relegation playoff
- **17th-18th place**: Relegated to Eerste Divisie

### Eerste Divisie (Tier 2)
- **Champion**: Promoted to Eredivisie
- **2nd-9th place**: Enter promotion playoffs (knockout format)
- **19th-20th place**: Relegated to Tweede Divisie

### Tweede Divisie (Tier 3)
- **Champion**: Promoted to Eerste Divisie
- **2nd-9th place**: Enter promotion playoffs (knockout format)
- **Bottom 2 teams**: Relegated to Derde Divisie

### Derde Divisie (Tier 4)
- **2 parallel divisions (Saturday & Sunday)**:
  - **Champions**: Promoted to Tweede Divisie
  - **2nd-5th place**: Enter promotion playoffs
  - **Bottom 2 teams**: Relegated to Vierde Klasse

### Vierde Klasse (Tiers 5-8)
- **8 regional divisions (4 Saturday, 4 Sunday)**:
  - **Champions**: Promoted to Derde Divisie
  - **2nd-5th place**: Enter promotion playoffs
  - **Bottom 2 teams**: Relegated to Vijfde Klasse

### Vijfde Klasse (Tier 9)
- **Multiple regional divisions**:
  - **Champions**: Promoted to Vierde Klasse
  - **2nd-5th place**: Enter promotion playoffs
  - No relegation (lowest tier)

## Promotion Playoff Format

### Eerste Divisie Playoffs (for Eredivisie)
1. **First Round**:
   - 2nd vs 9th
   - 3rd vs 8th
   - 4th vs 7th
   - 5th vs 6th
   - Two-legged ties (home & away)
2. **Semi-finals**:
   - Winners advance to single-match semi-finals
3. **Final**:
   - Single match at neutral venue
   - Winner qualifies for Eredivisie

### Lower Division Playoffs
1. **First Round**:
   - 2nd vs 5th
   - 3rd vs 4th
   - Single match at higher seed's home ground
2. **Final**:
   - Winners meet in single match at neutral venue
   - Winner earns promotion

## KNVB Cup Rules

### Qualification
- All professional clubs (Eredivisie & Eerste Divisie) enter in First Round
- Amateur clubs qualify through regional cup competitions
- 42 teams receive direct entry based on previous season's performance

### Tournament Format
- **First Round**: 64 teams (knockout)
- **Second Round**: 32 teams (knockout)
- **Round of 16**: 16 teams (knockout)
- **Quarter-finals**: 8 teams (knockout)
- **Semi-finals**: 4 teams (knockout)
- **Final**: Single match at De Kuip, Rotterdam

### European Qualification
- **Winner**: Qualifies for UEFA Europa League play-off round
- If winner already qualified for Europe via league position, the spot goes to the highest-placed Eredivisie team not already qualified for Europe

## Relegation Playoffs

### Eredivisie Relegation Playoff
- **16th place Eredivisie** vs **3rd-8th place Eerste Divisie**
- Two-legged tie
- Winner plays in Eredivisie next season

### Lower Division Relegation Playoffs
- Teams finishing just above relegation zone enter playoffs
- Format varies by division
- Typically involves home-and-away ties

## Tiebreakers

### League Positions
1. Points
2. Goal difference
3. Goals scored
4. Head-to-head points
5. Head-to-head goal difference
6. Head-to-head away goals
7. Playoff at neutral venue (if necessary for championship, relegation, or European qualification)

### Knockout Matches
1. Aggregate score
2. Away goals rule
3. Extra time (2x 15 minutes)
4. Penalty shootout (if necessary)

## Schedule
- **Eredivisie/Eerste Divisie**: August to May
- **Amateur Leagues**: September to May
- **KNVB Cup**: August to April
- **Winter Break**: Late December to mid-January (professional leagues only)
